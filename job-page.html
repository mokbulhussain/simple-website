Job Description

Hi there! We’re glad that you are taking a peek at our job board. Here at Axilweb, we are a passionate, fun-loving, growing team. We are looking for a Live Chat Support Agent like you to join our team and grow with us. 

We take on challenging projects, work creatively, and create new experiences for our customers.

Job Responsibilities

  • Respond quickly and accurately to inquiries via Live Chat.
  • Identifying customer needs and helping customers.
  • Handle multiple chats at once
  • Recognize and prioritize tasks arriving in a dedicated queue.
  • Chats answered within 30 seconds
  • Can take ownership of these issues and perform problem resolution to the customer’s satisfaction immediately
  • Be at your desk, ready and available at your scheduled times.
  • Be fully responsible and manage all activities relating to chat interactions
  • Drive continuous improvement opportunities by passing information from customers to the development team
  • Update reports regularly.
  • Provide training to new agents.

Primary Requirements

  • Strong written skills in English that lend to clear and concise text-based communication
  • Fast and accurate English typing skills
  • Preparing and updating FAQ
  • Ability to prepare reports from time to time for management
  • Be open to receiving feedback and constructive criticism
  • Be willing to help teammates, share knowledge and experience with them, and learn from them
  • Strong work ethic
